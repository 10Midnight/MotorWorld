About MotorWorld Corporation


Ang MotorWorld Corporation ay isa sa mga nangungunang motorcycle dealerships sa Pilipinas ngayon na may layunin na makapag hatid ng dekalidad at abot-kayang halaga ng motorsiklo, genuine parts. accessories, at langis. Nagsimula kami noong 2005 sa Bacolod City, Negros Occidental. Ngayon, ang aming network ay umaabot sa malalayong probinsya sa Luzon, Visayas, Mindanao. Hangad din namin ang makapag hatid ng mataas na antas ng customer service.

MotorWorld Corporation is one of the leading motorcycle dealerships in the Philippines and aims to provide customers with quality and affordable motorcycles, genuine parts, accessories, and oil. We started in 2005 in Bacolod City, Negros Occidenta. Our network now extends throughout numerous provinces in Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, as we continue to strive to deliver a high level of customer service.

In partnership with Robinsons Bank, we are able to deliver the best financial package to customers based on their needs and capabilities. We are dedicated to assisting Filipino merchants and employees in improving their quality of life in order to foster a more developed economy.
